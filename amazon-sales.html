01 / Overview

Decoding Amazon Sales Performance

This project delivers a high-impact Power BI dashboard built to evaluate sales performance, order statuses, customer payment habits, and geographic trends across multi-year Amazon store transactions.

By leveraging Power Query for heavy data transformation and DAX for key performance indicators, the dashboard translates raw transaction logs into clear, actionable business insights for e-commerce operators.

03 / Key Insights

Strategic Discoveries

Steady Yearly Revenue

Annual revenue consistently topped from 2020 through 2024, maintaining strong top-line sales stability across product categories.

Balanced Payment Options

Payment methods show an exact 20% split across Bank Transfer, Credit Card, Debit Card, Gift Card, and PayPal, indicating diverse customer preferences.

Category Distribution

Sports leads in volume (9,570 items), followed closely by Electronics (9,459) and Clothing (9,272), highlighting high-demand consumer sectors.

04 / Workflow

Data Architecture

01

Data Cleaning & Transformation

Imported raw Amazon sales records into Power Query. Standardized data types, handled missing values, and created calculated columns for order fulfillment status and delivery intervals.

02

DAX Measure Modeling

Engineered key metrics using DAX including Total Sales, Average Customer Rating, Average Delivery Days, Return Ratio, and Yearly Trend aggregations.

03

UI/UX & Dashboard Layout

Designed a sleek dark-themed dashboard UI incorporating intuitive slicers for Category, Payment Method, and Years, complemented by a cohesive gold/orange accent palette.
